Equestrian stables "Reitclub"

The Introduction

Equestrian stables "Reitclub" is located 20 minutes from the capital of Austria - Vienna. The base has 15 to 30 horses for riding. We can accommodate groups who want to ride together: beginners up to 8 people, advanced up to 12 people. We offer riding in nature - experiences that will leave lasting memories, anti-stress and relaxing walks for pleasure and relaxation /it is not necessary that you have ridden a horse!/
